Biography

Most websites fail not because they look bad, but because they ignore UX. And bad UX is bad business. That’s where I come in. I’m a Frontend Engineer who combines UI and UX, making me more than just a coder, I’m a UX Engineer. I focus on building websites and apps that people actually enjoy using and keep coming back to. I believe digital products don’t need endless features to succeed. Clean, thoughtful design and smooth interactions are what make them effective. Right now, I’m building my own apps: Nizebook(DocMaete): helping you save time and study smarter SnappSort: turning screenshot chaos into a premium, organized experience Beyond my projects, I work as a Growth Partner, helping founders and business owners turn ideas into websites, SaaS products, and digital experiences that truly work for their users and get monetized. If you’re ready to create digital experiences people actually enjoy, check out my feature section and book a call to work with me. Email: jasminedaniel046@gmail.com
